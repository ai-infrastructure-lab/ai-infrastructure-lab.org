
# People

::person{image="/images/headshot-al.jpg" name="Andreas Liesenfeld"}
__[Andreas Liesenfeld](https://www.ru.nl/personen/liesenfeld-a "Andreas Liesenfeld")__ directs the AI Infrastructure lab, co-founder of the [European Open Source AI Index](https://www.osai-index.eu) and Assistant Professor at the Centre for Language and Speech Technology (CLST), Radboud University Nijmegen where he researches the societal and economic implications of emerging AI technologies. His work focuses on technology assessment, open source AI, and AI industry research. At the Centre for Language and Speech Technology (CLST), Andreas Liesenfeld is a member of the language technology expertise group, occasionally organizes [AI Infrastructure walks](https://ai-infrastructure-lab.org/aiwalks), and, in collaboration with the Netherlands eScience center, develops open source research software for natural language processing.
::

::person{image="/images/headshot-db.jpg" name="Dick Blankvoort"}
__Dick Blankvoort__ is a researcher at the European Open Source AI Index. His primary interest lies in the field of Data Science, focusing both on the applications of generative AI and on their mathematical underpinnings. He is a computer science student, currently pursues his internship at OSAI, and teaches Computer Algebra.
::

::person{image="/images/headshot-arg.jpg" name="Arya Rahimi Golkhandan"}
__Arya Rahimi Golkhandan__ is a researcher at the European Open Source AI Index. His research interests are AI literary and the influence of AI on information ecosystems. He studies language science and technology at Radboud University and teaches a course on information science.
::
